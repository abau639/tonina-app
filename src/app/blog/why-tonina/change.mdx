# Why Tonina? Because Money Never Sleeps.

In the murky waters of the Amazon river, there swims a creature that defies logic. The **Tonina** (Amazon River Dolphin) is pink, ancient, and possesses a superpower that every anxious adult dreams of: *It never fully sleeps.*

## The Science of Half-Sleep

If a dolphin fell asleep like a human—fully unconscious—it would drown. Instead, they evolved **unihemispheric slow-wave sleep**. They shut down 50% of their brain to rest and repair, while the other 50% stays wide awake to surface for air and watch for predators.

## Why We Built This App

Your financial life is exactly like that river.
- The currents (**Inflation**) are always pushing against you.
- The predators (**Unexpected Expenses**) are always circling.

For years, financial advice has been: "Pay attention to everything, always." Track every latte. Watch every stock tick. It’s exhausting. It leads to burnout.

### Tonina is your other hemisphere.

We built this platform to be the 50% of the brain that stays awake. We track the depreciation of your car so you don't have to. We simulate the "what ifs" so you can sleep soundly knowing the river won't take you under.
